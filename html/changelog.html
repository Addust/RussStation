27 September 2020

AngryKnees and FlufflyCthulu updated:

  • Farting now has a 0.5 second hard cooldown, and a 2 second soft cooldown.

Fluffly Cthulu updated:

  • Visually updated the Lima Station emergency shuttle.
  • Space pirates have upgraded their fleets with advanced stealth equipment and have begun attacking stations unanounced.
  • Lima's turbine igniter now works, and there have been some layout changes in the turbine room.
  • Dwarves have reconnected with their roots and can now speak dwarvish.
  • Dwarves can no longer speak or understand galactic common.
  • Dwarves now use human sprites but inherently have the dwarfism gene.
  • Removed the old dwarf sprites.
  • replaced the forge sprite with a new one that more clearly conveys the purpose.
  • Fixed bad formatting in the spawner menu.
  • Gave adamantine ore the proper mineral datum, fixes runtimes and it now properly rewards points when placed in the orm.
  • Dwarves now have their own speech bubble icon, additionally added support for future bubble icons to be added.
  • Removed all roundstart active turfs in the dwarf ruin.
  • Redesigned the dwarven base, removed necropolis flooring and glowshrooms (the seeds are still available), and the ruin has a much cleaner design.
  • Added a unique suicide for skateboards.
  • Swapped the canister connections for Lima's SM.
  • Added a hotkey for suiciding. This hotkey will NOT ask for confirmation, use at your own risk.

HippaDilla updated:

  • Adds two cowboy hats
  • Adds several cowboy boots
  • Adds a deputy star accessory
  • Adds Big Iron cap gun
  • Adds Wild West crate

HippaDilla and AngryKnees updated:

  • Adds a silicon scream
  • Creates a framework for customizable farts
  • Farts now release gasses, depending on the type of fart and type of mob

Jureiia updated:

  • Added a new chem for clowning around Confettium.

Melbert updated:

  • Wetmore Slippery Signs! Construct them with a wet floor sign, a floor buffer upgrade, and a proximity sensor. Janitors and clowns can activate them in hand, and they will slip people running by. Can be emagged into...
  • Wetmore Slippery Signs Traitor Item! 7tc for 4 signs, and has a stock of 2. Janitor restricted. Slightly more aggressive then their normal counterparts.
  • DIY Slippery Sign kit to jani-drobes.

SQNZTB updated:

  • Medbay overhauled and rearranged. Now has psych office, breakroom, plumbing area.
  • Virology QOL enhancements: cable ties and moved the virus food dispenser.
  • Morgue now has /airlock/grunge doors for consistency with other stations.
  • Genetics moved back to science, now with a bottle of mutadone pills.
  • Many windows throughout science replaced with r-windows for consistency with other stations.
  • Atmospherics now has cans inside of the station tanks for visual reference.
  • Maintenance loot adjusted in coordination with the medical/science changes.
  • Contraband loot adjusted.
  • Added a paper to the bartending table for RussStation-specific drinks
  • Fixed xenobio's blast doors to work as expected.
